# PRD-052 Unit Conversion Plan

## Goal

Prevent demand and purchase workflows from silently producing zero or incorrect quantities/prices when BOM, stock, receipt, and purchase units differ without a valid conversion path.

## Scope

- Treat same-unit quantities as directly compatible.
- For different units, require both units to have positive `ConvertRateToBase` and matching normalized base unit codes.
- Surface missing conversion issues in material demand generation instead of silently falling back.
- Add data-quality `missing_conversion` issues for BOM, stock, and receipt/purchase reference-unit mismatches.
- Convert latest receipt price into the purchase/demand unit when generating purchase requests.
- Expose missing conversion counts in the frontend data-quality API/types and Admin Data cleanup KPI.
- Add regression tests for demand stock conversion, missing conversion issues, data-quality grouping, and purchase price conversion.
