<objective>
Add the minimum race-safe reconciliation-run provenance through one new forward migration, prove fresh and deterministic legacy upgrades, and close Phase 3 with a post-edit Gate A. No cleanup/apply mutation or old-surface retirement is permitted.
</objective>

<execution_context>

- Both empty-to-latest and legacy-to-latest execute successfully without old migration-file drift; destructive-DML review is scoped only to the newly generated provenance delta, because immutable historical migrations may legitimately contain historical data DML.
</must_haves>
